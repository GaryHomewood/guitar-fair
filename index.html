About

The North London Vintage Guitar Fair is the first of an annual event at the marvelous Mildmay Club bringing together dealers from in and around the London area and getting everyone under the same roof, with instruments of all vintages and price grades. As well as being a fantastic venue with two excellent subsidised bars, there will also be Q&A's with pro guitar players, organised by the author Martin Kelly, and a set up while-u-wait stand with Luthier Alberto from Creation Studios. Look forward to seeing you!
